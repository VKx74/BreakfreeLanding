Breakfree Trading Update: Minor Release

Minor release
Neural Agent 1.1.5
We're thrilled to introduce our latest feature: Auto Take Profit. The Neural Agent now employs a refined take-profit strategy when the market trades within a specified distance of your target take-profit level, while in profit.

How It Works:
When the market price comes within 9 pips of your designated take-profit point, the Neural Agent will automatically adjust the stop-loss a few points behind the current market price.

Benefits:
This ensures that the Neural Agent maximizes value from open positions, capturing gains even when the market narrowly misses your take-profit point.
