Frequently Asked Questions

What is the daily profit potential of Neural Agent?

The profit potential of Neural Agent is deeply intertwined with the activity and volatility of the financial markets. Its capacity to generate profit largely depends on the number of active currency pairs available to trade. There are times when the market is bustling, allowing us to engage in 8 to 16 trades simultaneously. However, during less active periods, we may only engage in 1 or 2 trades. Regardless of the market conditions, Neural Agent is designed to maximize profit potential within the existing market environment. On optimal days, Neural Agent is estimated to achieve a profit margin ranging from 0.1% to 2.5%.

What is the timeline for obtaining a VPS from us?

First of all, you do not need a VPS to run Neural Agent, but it does improve performance a lot by decreasing market latency. The process of acquiring a Virtual Private Server (VPS) from our service is typically within 8 hours. However, the exact duration may vary based on our current operational load.

Is it possible to utilize copy trading feature for multiple accounts?

Yes you are free to use a copy trader across multiple accounts, but it might not be ideal performance. Please notice all accounts using Neural Technology is subject to the 10% profit sharing program.

How does the 10% Profit Sharing Program work?

Our 10% Profit Sharing Program is designed as a win-win strategy for both our company and our clients. When you profit from our services, we benefit as well. Under this program, we only take 10% of the net profits generated through our software or trading strategies. It's important to note that this percentage is only applied to the profit earned, not the total trading capital. This ensures that our interests are directly aligned with your success in the markets.

We will generate an invoice on a monthly basis that you will receive in your inbox.
